1. App Structure
App is divided in to 2 main parts: Profile and Workout
2. Logging a Workout
You have 3 options:
  • Click quick workout option, and log your workout
  • Create a workout template, (so if you plan to reuse that workout structure)
  • Choose some of premeade workout templates
3. In-Workout Editing
Input your desired values and mark your sets as done
You can change set type by clicking on the letter on the type field
4. Managining Workouts
By default your workout templates will appear in Uncategorized split. (You can think of splits as folders for workout templates)
You can create your custom splits and drag desired workout templates there
5. Profile Section
In there you have 6 sections. This will become more useful after you workout for awhile:
  • Exercises - see your stats and how to perform them
  • Calendar - check your activity in calendar view
  • Statistics - check your stats for your activity
  • History - check all workouts you ever did
  • Goals - choose an exercise and your desired 1rm and track your progress
  • Achievements - check all achievements
